Hi, my name is Sanna and I have some advice for you!

Learn the basics very well

When you start coding, learn the basics very well. Don't rush into complicated coding too quickly.

Try to understand everything you learn and repeat similar code challenges multiple times.

Just don't give up!

Learning new things is hard. But you can do it! Just take it step by step. Even when you feel like you're going nowhere, you're actually building a steady foundation. Just don't give up.
